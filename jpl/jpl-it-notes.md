# JPL IT notes

#### USB-C monitor stops working on MacOS

1. Unplug monitor from wall
2. Unplug monitor from computer
3. Plug monitor in to computer
4. Plug monitor back in to wall

Note that steps 2 and 3 may be unnecessary
